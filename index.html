Lun Wang

I am a 4th-year PhD candidate at UC Berkeley, advised by Prof. Dawn Song. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree from Peking University in computer science in fall 2018.

I interned at Google during the summer of 2021, working with my fantastic hosts Kallista Bonawitz, Adria Gascon and Peter Kairouz.

I am affiliated with RISE Lab, BAIR Lab and Initiative for Cryptocurrencies & Contracts (IC3).

My research focuses on differential privacy. Currently, I mainly focus on the application of differential privacy in federated learning.
