Lun Wang

I am a staff research scientist at Google DeepMind, working on Gemini post-training (Memory, Tool Use, and Audio). I received my PhD in computer science from UC Berkeley, advised by Prof. Dawn Song in 2022. Previously, I earned my Bachelor's degree with honors in computer science from Peking University in 2018.
