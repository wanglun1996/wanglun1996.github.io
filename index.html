Bio

I am a 4th-year PhD candidate at UC Berkeley, advised by Prof. Dawn Song. I am fortunate to work closely with Prof. Joseph Near and Prof. Xinyu Xing. I am a member of RISE Lab and Initiative for Cryptocurrencies & Contracts (IC3). I received my Bachelor's degree from Peking University, Beijing, China. I am now an intern at Google, working with Kallista Bonawitz, Adria Gascon and Peter Kairouz. My research focuses on differential privacy. Currently, I mainly focus on the application of differential privacy in federated learning.
